10. A bird flying high in the air appears to be higher than in reality. Explain why?
Ans.
Bird flying in air is in the rarer medium and if we see it from denser medium than light
form bird refract towards the normal thus appears to come from the higher point. i.e.
Apparent height > Real height (BIRD APPEARS TO BE)
