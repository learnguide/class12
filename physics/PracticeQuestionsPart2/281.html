6.Two semiconductor materials A and B shown in the figure are made by doping
germanium crystal with arsenic and indium respectively. The two are joined end to end
and connected to a battery as shown.
(a) Will the junction be forward biased or reverse biased? Justify
(b) Sketch a V-I graph for this arrangement
Ans.
Material A is n-type as it is doped with pentavalent impurity and material B is p-type as
it is doped with trivalent impurity. As a result the junction becomes reverse biased because
positive terminal of the battery is connected to n-type and negative terminal to the p-type
hence it is reversed biased.
