5 Mark Questions
1.Distinguish between conductors, insulators and semiconductors on the basis of
energy band diagrams?
Ans.
Conductor
– Conduction band in a conductor is either partially filled or conduction and
valence band overlaps each other. There is no energy gap in a conductor.
Insulators
– conduction band and valence band of all insulator are widely separated by and
energy gap of the order 6 to 9eV Also conduction band of an insulator is almost empty.
Semiconductor
– In semiconductors the energy gap is very small i.e. about 1ev only.
