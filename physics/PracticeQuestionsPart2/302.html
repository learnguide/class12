1.Name the type of the communication system in which the signal is discrete and binary

Ans.

2.What is the purpose of modulating a signal in transmission?
Ans.
Modulation is done because low frequency signal cannot be transmitted to a longer
distance so in order to increase the range of transmission modulation is done.

the earth?
Ans.
The transmitting and receiving antenna must be in the line of sight.
