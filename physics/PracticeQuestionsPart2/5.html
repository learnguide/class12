1. What are optical fibres? Give their one use?
Ans.
Optical fibres consist of thin and long strands of fine quality glass or quartz coated with
a thin layer of material of refractive index less than the refractive index of strands. They
work on the principle of total internal reflection so they do not suffer any loss.
Uses
The optical fibres are used in medical investigations i.e. one can examine the inside view of
stomach and intestine by a method called endoscopy.
