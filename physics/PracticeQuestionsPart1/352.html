4. IF the earth did not have atmosphere would its average surface temperature be
higher or lower than what it is now?
Ans.
The infra-red radiations get trapped inside the earth’s atmosphere due to green house
effect which makes the earth warm. Therefore average temperature of the earth would have
been low.
