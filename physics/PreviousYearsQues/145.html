7. Why is the use of a.c. voltage preferred over d.c. voltage ? Give two reasons.
Solution:
Any two
of the following (or any other correct) reasons :
i. AC can be transmitted with much lower energy losses as compared to DC
ii. AC voltage can be adjusted (stepped up or stepped down) as per requirement.
iii. AC current in a circuit can be controlled using (almost) wattles devices like the choke coil.
iv. AC is easier to generate.
