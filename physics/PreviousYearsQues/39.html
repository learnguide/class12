18.
Draw a block diagram of a generalized communication system. Write the functions of each of the
following
(a) Transmitter
(b) Channel
(c) Receiver
Solution:
Block diagram of communication system :
Transmitter :- A transmitter is an arrangement that converts the message signal to a form suitable for
transmission and then transmits it through some suitable communication channel.
Channel :- Channel is the medium through which the signal is transmitted for transmitter to receiver.
Receiver :- A receiver extracts the desired message signals from the received signals at the channel output.
