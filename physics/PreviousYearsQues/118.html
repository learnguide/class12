Section A
1. What is the function of a band pass filter used in a modulator for obtaining AM signal ?
Solution:
The output produced by square law device is passed to band pass filter which rejects the dc and the sinusoids
of frequencies wm, 2wm and 2wc and retains the frequencies wc, wc
–
wm and wc + wm. The output of
band pass filter is an AM wave.
2.
A planar loop of rectangular shape is moved within the region of a uniform magnetic field acting
perpendicular to its plane. What is the direction and magnitude of the current induced in it ?
Solution:
The magnetic flux linked with a circuit is not changing with time so there will be no induced current in the
loop.
