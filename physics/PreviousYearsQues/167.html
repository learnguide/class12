6. How does the mutual inductance of a pair of coils change when
(i) distance between the coils is decreased and
(ii) number of turns in the coils is decreased ?
Solution:
(i) Mutual inductance increased on decreasing distance.
(ii) Mutual inductance decreased on decreasing the number of turns.
7. Define the activity of a given radioactive substance. Write its S.I. units.
Solution:
Activity : Rate of disintegration of radiative substance. i.e. number of radioactive nuclei disintegrating in
unit time is called activity.
SI unit :-
1 disintegration per second (dps) OR 1 Bq.
