Three circuits, each consisting of a switch 'S' and two capacitors, are initially charged, as shown in the
figure. After the switch has been closed, in which circuit will the charge on the left-hand capacitor (i)
increase, (ii) decrease and (iii) remains same ? Give reasons.
Solution:
When charged capacitors are connected to each other then the charge will flow from the capacitor with
higher potential towards the capacitor with lower potential untill a common potential is reached.
(a) In fig. (a) the potential of both the capacitor is same so the charge on left hand capacitor remains the
same
(b) In fig. (b) the potential of left hand capacitor is high so charge from 6Q to 3Q. Therefore charge on left
hand capacitor will decrease.
(c) In fig. (c) the potential of left hand capacitor is low so charge will flow from 3Q to 6Q. Therefore charge
on left hand capacitor will increase.
